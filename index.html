What is Robotics?

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that combines engineering, computer science, and technology to design, build, and operate robots. Robots are machines that can perform tasks autonomously or with minimal human intervention. They can be programmed to carry out a wide range of activities, from simple repetitive tasks to complex operations in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and space exploration.
